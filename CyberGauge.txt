-This was a tool made in conjunction with the Governance team and the Development team.

- Data was extracted from the MSSQL DB and using stored procedures it was processed and fed to the front end

- The data on the front end was represented in various visual representations like bar charts, line graphs, pie charts and combo graphs

- With this the security controls of Infosys were brought onto a single platform and was presentable to not only the managers,
  but was regularly presented to the CISO in order to gauge the compliance of the polilcies with the controls present

- This highlighted the gaps in security policies and even highlighted workflows which hindered achieving the security policy goal

- This tool helped achieve NIST and ISO 27001 standards in Infosys

-Furthermore, this tool was finalized as a finished product called "CyberGaze" and was marketed to generate revenue for the company
